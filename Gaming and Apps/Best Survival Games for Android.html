Best Survival Games for Android (Offline & Online)

Looking for the best survival games for Android? Whether you want to play offline in peace or go online with friends, these mobile games deliver thrilling survival challenges, exploration, crafting, and base-building. Below, we’ve separated the top offline and online survival games for Android you can play today.

Offline Survival Games for Android

1. Minecraft: Pocket Edition

Minecraft: Pocket Edition is one of the best offline survival games for Android mobile. Players can explore vast randomly generated worlds, mine resources, craft tools, and build shelters — all without an internet connection. The mobile version runs smoothly on most Android devices and provides a full survival experience offline.

In this mobile survival game, you’ll gather wood, stone, and ores to craft weapons and tools that help you survive the night against dangerous mobs like zombies and skeletons. The game supports full offline gameplay, allowing Android players to build endlessly even without Wi-Fi. Minecraft’s pixelated charm, simple controls, and deep crafting system make it one of the best Android survival games available today.

  • Offline survival and creative modes.
  • Endless crafting and exploration on mobile.
  • Optimized performance for Android devices.

2. Day R Survival

Day R Survival is a post-apocalyptic offline Android survival game where you must stay alive in a world devastated by nuclear war. With no internet needed, you’ll navigate through radioactive wastelands, scavenge supplies, and craft weapons. This mobile survival RPG mixes realism and strategy — you’ll manage hunger, thirst, and fatigue while exploring hundreds of locations.

Perfect for Android users who prefer deep, story-driven gameplay offline, Day R Survival offers crafting, trading, and character leveling. Its detailed map and survival mechanics make it one of the best offline survival games for Android with realistic challenges and an immersive world to explore.

  • Offline play with full survival mechanics.
  • Realistic health, hunger, and crafting systems.
  • Huge world map with multiple story missions.

3. Don't Starve: Pocket Edition

Don't Starve: Pocket Edition is a top-tier offline mobile survival game designed for Android players seeking a real challenge. The game drops you into a strange wilderness full of strange creatures and magic. You must gather resources, craft tools, and survive as long as possible without any online connection.

This game combines unique hand-drawn art with deep gameplay. The hunger system, crafting mechanics, and tough AI enemies make it one of the best survival games for Android offline. It’s smooth, optimized for mobile, and offers hours of challenging gameplay even without internet access.

  • Fully offline Android gameplay.
  • Challenging hunger and health systems.
  • Distinct art style and high difficulty.

4. Ocean Is Home: Island Life Sim

Ocean Is Home is an immersive offline Android survival simulator where you’re stranded on a deserted island. You’ll need to gather wood, stone, and food to build a shelter, craft tools, and survive the elements. The realistic environment and day-night cycle make it one of the best mobile survival games offline.

With smooth controls and simple mechanics, it runs perfectly on Android phones. This survival experience doesn’t need an internet connection, making it ideal for players who want to explore and build in peace on mobile devices.

  • Offline sandbox survival on Android.
  • Crafting, building, and resource gathering.
  • Beautiful open island environment.

Online Survival Games for Android

5. Last Day on Earth: Survival

Last Day on Earth is one of the most popular online survival games for Android mobile. In this zombie apocalypse world, you must collect materials, craft weapons, and build a base to defend yourself against other survivors and hordes of the undead. The online multiplayer system allows you to team up or fight against players globally.

Its smooth performance on Android devices and deep crafting system make it one of the best online mobile survival games. You can play with friends, trade resources, and complete missions while upgrading your weapons and armor.

  • Online multiplayer and PvP gameplay.
  • Craft weapons, build bases, and fight zombies.
  • Regular events and global leaderboards.

6. ARK: Survival Evolved

ARK: Survival Evolved brings the epic open-world survival experience to Android mobile. As an online multiplayer game, you’ll explore a massive island filled with dinosaurs, craft tools, and build bases while surviving dangerous environments. The Android version runs surprisingly well, offering a true console-level survival experience.

Its online community is huge, and you can team up with other players to form tribes, hunt, and dominate. ARK is one of the best online survival games for Android thanks to its stunning graphics, crafting systems, and massive multiplayer world.

  • Online multiplayer open-world survival.
  • Tame dinosaurs, craft, and explore.
  • High-quality graphics optimized for Android.

7. LifeAfter

LifeAfter is a visually stunning online mobile survival game set in a world infested with zombies. This Android game focuses on survival, exploration, and teamwork. You can craft weapons, build shelters, and explore large open areas while connecting with players globally. The detailed graphics and storyline make it one of the best survival games for Android online.

With cooperative gameplay, trading, and missions, LifeAfter delivers an MMO-style experience on mobile. It’s perfect for Android players who love immersive graphics and deep survival elements.

  • Massive online multiplayer survival.
  • Crafting, trading, and exploration missions.
  • Realistic visuals optimized for Android.

8. Rust: Mobile Edition

Rust: Mobile Edition brings one of the toughest PC survival experiences to Android. This online survival game challenges you to survive in a world full of players, each trying to stay alive and dominate. Build bases, craft weapons, and raid others in this intense PvP-focused environment.

It runs smoothly on modern Android phones and offers full online functionality. The realistic combat and community-driven mechanics make Rust: Mobile one of the best online survival games for Android players looking for competition and strategy.

  • Online PvP sandbox gameplay.
  • Craft weapons, build bases, and raid others.
  • Dynamic environment and player-driven world.

How to Choose the Right Survival Game for Android

To pick the right survival game for your Android device, think about your play style. If you prefer solo, pick offline survival games like Ocean Is Home or Don't Starve. If you like multiplayer battles and cooperative play, go for online titles like Last Day on Earth or LifeAfter. Also ensure your Android hardware can run the game and check whether the game requires constant internet or can run partly offline.

Final Thoughts on the Best Survival Games for Android

With these **offline and online survival games**, you get a full range of experiences — from peaceful solo crafting to high-stakes multiplayer survival. Download a few, test them out, and see which ones match your style. Survive, build, fight, and enjoy the best **Android survival games** in 2025.
