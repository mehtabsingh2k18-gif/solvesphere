When you visit SolveSphere, we may collect two types of information:

We use the information we collect to:

Our email subscription service is powered by Mailchimp. When you subscribe, your email address is stored securely on Mailchimp’s servers. You can unsubscribe anytime by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any email we send you. We do not sell or share your email address with third parties.

  • Remember your preferences for a smoother browsing experience.
  • Track anonymous analytics data via tools like Google Analytics.
  • Measure the performance of our articles and improve content strategy.

You can disable cookies anytime in your browser settings, but some features of the website may not work as intended.

We may use third-party services to enhance functionality. These include:

We take reasonable steps to keep your data secure. Your personal data is transmitted over secure (HTTPS) connections and stored with industry-standard protection measures. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
