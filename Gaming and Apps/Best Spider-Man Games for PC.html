Best Spider-Man Games for PC Under 10GB in 2025

Discover the top Spider-Man games for PC that take less than 10GB. Full system requirements, gameplay details, and download size for low-storage PCs in 2025.

Why Choose Spider-Man Games Under 10GB?

Not all PCs have high storage. Lightweight Spider-Man games allow you to enjoy thrilling superhero adventures without filling your hard drive. Perfect for gamers with low-end PCs or limited storage.

Top Spider-Man Games for PC Under 10GB

Top Spider-Man Games for PC (Under 10GB)

1. Spider-Man: Shattered Dimensions

Size: 9.5GB

Genre: Action, Adventure

System Requirements: Windows 7/8/10, 2GB RAM, Intel Core 2 Duo, DirectX 9.0c, 512MB GPU

Spider-Man: Shattered Dimensions lets players explore four different Spider-Man universes. Each universe offers unique gameplay styles and art designs, keeping the experience fresh and exciting. Players battle villains from different realities while mastering combos, acrobatics, and web-based attacks. The game’s lightweight size ensures smooth performance on mid-range PCs. Collectibles, unlockable costumes, and environmental interactions make this a highly replayable action-adventure game suitable for Spider-Man fans looking for a rich experience under 10GB.

2. Spider-Man: Edge of Time

Size: 8.7GB

Genre: Action, Adventure

System Requirements: Windows 7/8/10, 2GB RAM, Intel Core 2 Duo, DirectX 9.0c, 512MB GPU

Edge of Time introduces a time-travel storyline where players control two Spider-Men across different timelines. Actions in the past affect the future, adding a strategic layer to missions. The game offers parkour-based navigation, web-swinging, and engaging combat sequences. Despite its lightweight 8.7GB size, the game delivers compelling visuals, story-driven missions, and hidden collectibles. It’s perfect for gamers seeking dual-timeline adventures without large storage requirements.

3. Spider-Man: Web of Shadows

Size: 9.8GB

Genre: Action, RPG

System Requirements: Windows 7/8/10, 2GB RAM, Intel Core 2 Duo, DirectX 9.0c, 512MB GPU

Web of Shadows blends open-world exploration, action, and RPG mechanics. Players face an alien symbiote invasion and can swing freely across New York City. The game includes fluid combat combos, moral choice mechanics, and engaging side missions. Lightweight for its features, it offers smooth gameplay on mid-range PCs. The dynamic soundtrack, hidden collectibles, and character upgrades make Web of Shadows a deep and replayable Spider-Man experience under 10GB.

4. Spider-Man: Friend or Foe

Size: 7.5GB

Genre: Action, Co-op

System Requirements: Windows XP/7, 1.5GB RAM, Intel Pentium 4, DirectX 9.0c

Friend or Foe focuses on co-op gameplay and fun missions. Players team up with allies and even former villains to complete levels. The game features simplified combat, puzzles, and collectible rewards. Its lightweight 7.5GB installation ensures smooth play on older PCs. Designed for casual and younger audiences, it’s ideal for gamers seeking a cooperative Spider-Man adventure with easy accessibility and replay value.

Recommended Spider-Man Games for Higher-End PCs (Under 20GB)

These games are larger in size but provide enhanced graphics, modern gameplay, and more immersive experiences. Suitable for users with higher-spec PCs who want the ultimate Spider-Man experience.

1. Spider-Man: Miles Morales

Size: 15GB

Genre: Action, Adventure

System Requirements: Windows 10/11, 8GB RAM, Intel Core i5-11400, NVIDIA GTX 1660, DirectX 12

Miles Morales showcases stunning graphics, fast-paced combat, and unique abilities like bio-electric venom blasts and invisibility. The story follows Miles as he protects New York City, offering a narrative-driven adventure with side quests, collectibles, and dynamic web-swinging. Though larger in size, it’s optimized for modern PCs and delivers a next-gen Spider-Man experience under 20GB.

2. Spider-Man: Ultimate Edition (Remastered)

Size: 18GB

Genre: Action, Adventure

System Requirements: Windows 10/11, 8GB RAM, Intel Core i5-10400, NVIDIA GTX 1660 Super, DirectX 12

The Ultimate Edition remaster combines classic Spider-Man gameplay with enhanced graphics, all DLCs, and side quests. Players enjoy fluid web-swinging, strategic combat, and a complete storyline in an open-world New York City. Designed for higher-end PCs, this 18GB game offers an immersive experience for players who want more detailed visuals and extra content than lightweight versions provide.

Gameplay Tips

  • Use webs strategically for traversal and combat efficiency.
  • Upgrade Spider-Man’s abilities for enhanced attacks.
  • Collect hidden items and unlock bonus suits for extra rewards.
  • For low-end PCs, reduce shadows, textures, and close background apps for smooth gameplay.
  • Explore each game’s unique mechanics and side missions for maximum enjoyment.

FAQ — Spider-Man PC Games

  • Can I play these games on Windows 10? Yes, all games listed are compatible with Windows 7, 8, and 10.
  • Do these games require high-end GPUs? No, they are optimized for low-end PCs under 10GB.
  • Can I play them offline? Yes, most titles have offline single-player modes.
  • Are there multiplayer modes? Only select titles like “Friend or Foe” support co-op gameplay.
